Hi! I am an industrial PhD student at AstraZeneca affiliated with KTH, supervised by Jon Paul Janet (main industry) and Hossein Azizpour (main KTH), with co-supervision from Ola Engkvist, Alessandro Tibo and Patrick Bryant.

I work on generative models for structural based drug design, trying to push the boundaries of what's possible with AI. Outside my research, I am very passionate about computer vision, and a true lover of music and photography. Feel free to get in touch if you want to collaborate, or just say hi at riccardo.tedoldi@gmail.com.
